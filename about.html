About Our College

Empowering Women Engineers Since 2000

Bharati Vidyapeeth's College of Engineering for Women

Established in June 2000, our college was founded with the collective efforts of experts having long and outstanding academic experience in professional education.

We emphasize training deserving young women who have an ambition to pursue higher education in engineering and are desirous of acquiring skills to meet the demands of fast-growing industries, technical education, and research facilities.

Our mission is 'Social transformation through dynamic education' - a commitment that drives everything we do, from curriculum design to campus culture.
